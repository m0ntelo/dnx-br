Stages of App Modernisation
You have apps running on a time-proven platform that might meet your users requirements but is costing too much to maintain and slowing down delivery of new features.
REPLATFORMING
Your apps a new home. We start by deploying our platform, we like to call it DNX.one as it's the first layer of a modernisation, the terrain where your application will flourish.
DNX.one is a secure, high-available, low-cost and scalable container platform. It combines years of cloud experience in a platform completely built with simplicity in mind, infrastructure-as-code, open-source technologies, and designed for AWS with well-architected principles.
